About NewJeans

NewJeans is a 5 member girl Kpop group. The names of the members are Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. This group has recently become very popular thanks to their recent hit songs called 'OMG' and 'Ditto'. This band is still fairly new as they only released their first debut single back in July 22, 2022.
