Drake's Career

Drake the Toronto-born native started music with his first studio album Thank Me Later (2010) which featured additions from Alica keys, Lil Wayne, and Kid Cudi. With the release of 7 studio albums throughout his career, he has amassed a cult following with dedicated fans. Drake is known as one of the most influential artists of the 21st century through his artistic touch in music and fashion.
